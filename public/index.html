ABOUT INVASIVE DETECTION
Looking for your local environmental analysis? Select a group and upload an aerial map photo to the Customize Your Analysis box below.

Each year, ecosystems face challenges from non-native species that can alter habitats, deplete resources, and disrupt local wildlife. Our native regions log roughly 100,000 observations of disruptive plants, spanning over 5,000 distinct floodplains and drought areas. Invasive plant events account for an average of approximately 650 major ecological shifts. W.E.S.L.E.Y. provides analysis, hydrologic context, and environmental forecasts for the United States for the protection of native life and enhancement of natural areas.
